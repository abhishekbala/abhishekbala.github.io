Pradosha Murthy Enraikku Siva Krupai

Description

Pradosha is a bimonthly occasion on the thirteenth day of every forthnight in the Hindu calendar. The auspicious 3 hour period, 1.5 hours before and after the sunset is one of the optimum time for worship of Lord Shiva.

The song Enraikku Siva Krupai in Raga Mukhari and Misra Chapu Talam is a composition of Neelakanta Sivan.

When will I receive Lord Shiva’s grace? When will this wretched person’s grief cease? When there is wealth, all kith and kin come to praise and serve. When there is a reduction of wealth they do not speak even when they see us! When will I receive Lord Shiva’s grace to push away this wicked and villainous world and get liberation?
