Muruga & Ganesha doing Shiva Pooja

Muruga

Legend has it that during the churning of the oceans by the devas and asuras, in addition to the holy nectar, the poison Halahala rose from the ocean. To save the world from the poison, Shiva consumed it. When the poison was going down his throat, Parvati stopped it there by tightly holding it, and thus his throat turned blue.
