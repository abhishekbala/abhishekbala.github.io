Dattatreya Dattatreya Trimurthi Rupa

Description

Dattatreya is a paradigmatic Sannyasi (monk) and one of the lords of Yoga in Hinduism. In many regions of India and Nepal, he is considered a deity. A typical icon of Dattatreya shows the three heads of Brahma, Vishnu, and Siva, representing the Trimurthi.

The song Dattatreya Trimurthi Rupa in Raga Ranjani and Misra Chapu Talam is a composition of Sri Ganapathi Sadchidananda. The song was popularized by the famous musician Palakkad K.V. Narayanaswamy.
