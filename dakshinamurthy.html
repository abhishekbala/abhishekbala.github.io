Dakshinamurthy

Description

Dakshinamurthy is an aspect of the Hindu god Shiva as a guru or teacher of all types of knowledge. This form of Shiva is a teacher of yoga, music, and wisdom.

We pray to Dakshinamurthy with various slokams. The famous Dakshinamurthy Stotram by Adi Sankaracharya is a hymn that goes as:

om namah pranavarthaya shuddhajnanaikamurtaye !
nirmalaya prashantaya dakshinamurtaye namaha

Muthuswami Dikshitar has composed Sri Dakshinamurthe in Raga Shankarabharanam set to Misra Jhampa Talam.
