63 Nayanmargal Thevaram

Description

The Nayanars were a group of 63 saints in the 6th to 8th century who were devoted to Shiva in Tamil Nadu. They heavily influenced the Bhakti movement in Tamil.

The three most prominent Nayanars were Sambandar, Thirunavukkarasar, and Sundarar. The first seven volumes of their works are known as Thevaram. The singing of Thevaram continues as a common practice in many Shiva temples of Tamil Nadu today.

Thodudaiyya Sevian in Raga Gambheera Nattai is a Thevaram composition of Thirugnanasambandar.
